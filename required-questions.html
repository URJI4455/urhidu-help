Section 06 — Final Section

General Requirements

Providing clear business assets, reference links, and timeline expectations helps our team keep your project on schedule while ensuring your site stays legally compliant and built for future growth.

You're Almost Done

This is the final section of the questionnaire. It covers five remaining elements: social proof assets, social media and inspiration links, Google Business and mobile app plans, launch timeline and budget, and legal page requirements.

1. Testimonials, Partner Logos & Photo Galleries

Gather positive reviews from past clients, logos of companies you serve, or original workspace photos. Online users buy from brands they trust. These assets allow us to build custom Social Proof Sections that dramatically boost customer conversion rates.

Client Testimonials

Highly Recommended

Written reviews, star ratings, or video testimonials from satisfied clients. We use these to build review carousels and quote sections on your homepage. Provide: client name, company (if applicable), their quote, and a photo if available.

Partner / Client Logos

Optional

Logos of notable companies you have worked with or serve. These form a "trusted by" logo grid strip — one of the highest-converting trust signals on any professional website. Provide in PNG (transparent) or SVG format.

Workspace / Product Photos

Optional

Original, high-resolution photographs of your team, workspace, products, or services in action. Authentic photography dramatically outperforms stock images in building user trust. Minimum 1200px wide, JPG or PNG format.

Social & Inspiration

2. Social Media Links & Inspiration Websites

Share the web addresses of your social media profiles and list 1 or 2 existing websites you admire. This allows us to program social icons that connect your website directly to your online communities, and understand your exact design preferences.

Social Media Profiles

Optional

Provide full URLs to each platform your business uses actively. We support: Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, Telegram, YouTube, LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Snapchat, and others.

Format Example
Facebook:   https://facebook.com/yourpage
Instagram:  https://instagram.com/yourhandle
Telegram:   https://t.me/yourchannel
YouTube:    https://youtube.com/@yourchannel
LinkedIn:   https://linkedin.com/company/yourco

Inspiration Websites

Highly Recommended

Share 1–3 URLs of existing websites whose design, layout, or navigation you love. Inspiration links show us your exact visual preferences so we can align our design direction before we write a single line of code. Be as specific as possible — "I love how their homepage hero section is structured" is very helpful.

Google & Mobile Apps

3. Google Business Profile & Future Mobile Apps

Indicate if you need a Google Maps directory profile set up, and let us know if you plan to build a mobile app later. These decisions affect how we architect your website's backend.

Google Business Profile

Recommended for Local Businesses

A Google Business profile ensures your business shows up on Google Maps and local search when customers search nearby. We can create or link your existing profile and embed an interactive map widget directly into your Contact page.

What Google Business Profile Requires

To verify a Google Business profile, you typically need a physical business address or phone number. Virtual addresses may not be accepted. Our team will guide you through the verification process.

Future Mobile App Plans

Optional

Let us know if you plan to build a mobile app in the future. Knowing this early tells our engineers to build your website with flexible API connections — making it fast and cost-effective to feed the same data to a mobile app later, without rebuilding your backend from scratch.

Launch Date & Budget

State your launch deadline and your investment boundaries. Timelines and budgets help us assign the right development resources to hit your target on time and within scope.

Timeline What We Deliver Best For
Urgent (Under 2 weeks) Professional single-page "coming soon" landing page launched first. Full site built in parallel. Businesses needing an online presence immediately (events, product launches)
Standard (2–6 weeks) Complete multi-page website with all requested features, fully tested and launched. Most small-to-medium business websites
Enterprise (6–12 weeks) Complex platforms with custom databases, e-commerce, booking systems, and API integrations. Hotels, large e-commerce stores, corporate platforms
Flexible (No fixed date) We work at a measured pace focused on quality over speed, with regular milestone check-ins. Startups and new businesses building their first platform
Urgent Launch?

State your deadline clearly. For highly urgent launches, our team can deploy a professional, branded "coming soon" landing page first — so your business remains active online — while your full website is built in the background.

Legal Pages

5. Legal Pages

Selecting legal pages signals our team to deploy the appropriate legal text containers, protecting your business from data privacy liabilities and securing your online checkout systems.

Privacy Policy

Strongly Recommended

Required by law if your site uses analytics (e.g., Google Analytics) or collects user data via contact forms. Informs users how their data is collected and used. Protects your business from regulatory fines under GDPR and similar frameworks.

Terms of Service

Recommended for E-Commerce

Sets the legal agreement between your business and users of your website. Especially critical for e-commerce stores, booking platforms, and any site handling financial transactions. Defines refund policies, service conditions, and dispute procedures.

Cookie Policy

Optional

Discloses to users how your website uses browser cookies for analytics, session management, and advertising tracking. Required by EU law for any site serving European visitors. We include a cookie consent banner if selected.

Frequently Asked Questions

If your website uses basic analytics (like Google Analytics) to track visitor numbers, or has a standard contact form that collects a name and email address, privacy laws in many countries require you to inform users how their browsing data is collected and processed. Having a Privacy Policy page protects your brand from legal fines and builds trust with visitors who are increasingly privacy-conscious.

Please state your deadline clearly in the questionnaire. For highly urgent launches, our development team can build your project in phases — launching a professional, single-page "coming soon" landing page first, then building out your secondary pages while your business remains active online. This means you can have a live, branded web presence within days while the full site is completed.

For pure informational websites, a Terms of Service page is not strictly required. However, it is still recommended if your site allows user registration, forum participation, or any kind of user-generated content. For any website handling payments or bookings, Terms of Service is essential and protects you from financial disputes and chargebacks.

URHIDU Company provides professionally structured legal page templates tailored to your specific business type and data collection practices. These templates cover all standard regulatory requirements. For highly specialized industries (fintech, healthcare, legal services) with complex data handling requirements, we recommend having a local legal professional review the final content before publishing.
