Section 04

Target Audience

Your target audience consists of the specific individuals most likely to use your website. Defining who they are ensures our team builds a site that feels natural, loads correctly on their devices, and speaks their language.

Who Is Your Most Valuable Visitor?

Focus on the person most likely to make a purchasing decision or take a meaningful action — not everyone who might stumble onto your site. This is who we design for.

1. Age Range of Primary Audience

Specify the main age bracket of your ideal user. Age directly affects website usability and accessibility decisions our team makes throughout the design process.

Age Group Design Approach Example Audience
16 – 25 Bold visuals, fast interactions, minimalist menus, social media-style layouts Fashion brand, music service, gaming platform
25 – 40 Clean layouts, efficiency-focused UX, clear CTAs, professional tone Corporate service, fintech, SaaS, consulting
35 – 55 Larger text, explicit labels, trust signals, simplified navigation Legal firm, medical clinic, real estate agency
55 – 65+ Large fonts, prominent buttons, high contrast, step-by-step instruction layout Healthcare provider, government service, retirement planning
All Ages Universally accessible: WCAG-compliant contrast, clear hierarchy, flexible text sizing Supermarket, community service, utility provider
Focus on Decision-Makers

If you operate a family dental clinic, your primary website visitors are the adult billing decision-makers (ages 25–60) who book appointments — not the children who receive the actual service. Design for the person who types in your URL.

Geographic Focus

2. Geographic Focus

Select where your primary visitors physically live. This determines how our engineers configure your website's server infrastructure and content delivery.

Local

A specific city or neighborhood (e.g., Dire Dawa, Addis Ababa). We host your website files on nearby servers for instant loading speeds.

National

An entire single country. We configure multi-region server routing to ensure fast loading speeds across all major cities in your country.

International

Multiple global regions or countries. We install a Global Content Delivery Network (CDN) to ensure the site loads quickly anywhere in the world — critical for hotels, export businesses, and international service providers.

Language

3. Language Spoken by Audience

List all the languages your website must support. This tells our developers how many translation frameworks to build into your content management system (CMS).

Typography Consideration

Language selection also affects typography. Fonts must cleanly support all selected alphabets and scripts. For Ethiopian markets, we ensure full support for Amharic (Ethiopic script), Afan Oromo (Latin), and English across all heading and body typefaces.

Language Combination Development Implication
English only Single-language CMS. Standard Western font stack with Latin script support.
Amharic + English Bilingual CMS with RTL/LTR toggle support and Ethiopic script font loading.
Amharic + Afan Oromo + English Multi-language CMS with language switcher component and full script coverage.
English + Arabic Bilingual CMS with full RTL (right-to-left) layout mirroring for Arabic pages.
Primary Device

4. Primary Device Used

Indicate how you expect your audience to access your site. Every site we build works on all devices, but this setting changes our design priority and development focus.

Mobile First

We spend extra development time optimizing for single-thumb scrolling, touch responsiveness, and light asset loading. Navigation is simplified to 3–4 top-level items. All CTAs are large and easy to tap.

Desktop First

We prioritize larger layouts, expanded navigation menus, hover effects, and richer visual content. Ideal for corporate B2B platforms, SaaS dashboards, and professional service firms where users are office-based.

Both (Responsive)

We build a fully adaptive layout that seamlessly transitions across all screen sizes. The most common recommendation for businesses serving a general audience — ensures no visitor is left with a broken experience.
