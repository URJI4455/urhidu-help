Section 02

Contact Information

Your contact details are split into two strict categories: what gets displayed publicly on your website, and what remains strictly confidential for internal project coordination.

Two Categories of Contact Details

To protect your privacy and build an effective website, our form splits your contact details into Public Website Details and Internal Project Administration. Read each section carefully before submitting.

1. Public Website & Customer Support Details

This information is embedded directly into your website source code. It acts as your public consumer support infrastructure — visible to all visitors.

Public Support Email

Required

Receives all automated website contact form entries and user help tickets. This is displayed publicly and used for direct customer inquiries.

Primary Phone (Public Hotline)

Required

Displayed as a clickable link in your website header and footer for immediate customer calls. On mobile, tapping this number opens the phone dialer directly.

Alternative Phone

Optional

Listed on your contact page so users can reach you if your primary line is busy or unavailable.

Instant Support Channel (Telegram / WhatsApp)

Optional

Powers direct-chat floating widgets on your web pages for real-time mobile customer support. Highly recommended for Ethiopian businesses where Telegram and WhatsApp are primary communication tools.

Physical Address

Required

Provide: Street, Building, City, Country, and Postal Code. This configures your interactive Google Maps widget and local SEO data — critical for businesses with a physical location.

Address Format 
Street / Building Name
City, Region
Country
Postal Code (if applicable)
Internal Administration

2. Internal Project Administration

This information remains strictly confidential. It is used exclusively behind the scenes by our development team for project coordination, approvals, and deployment notifications.

Confidential Information

Internal contact details are never displayed on the public website. They are used only for wireframe demonstrations, milestone sign-offs, and development coordination.

Primary Decision Maker Name & Role

RequiredConfidential

Sets the legal project champion who holds final approval authority on design layouts and milestone sign-offs. Provide their full name and role (Owner, Manager, Director, etc.).

Direct Administrative Email & Phone

RequiredConfidential

Used for critical system notifications, private wireframe demonstrations, and project scope discussions. This should be a direct line to the decision maker — not a general inbox.

Preferred Internal Chat Channel

OptionalConfidential

Used for rapid development coordination, instant progress screenshots, and deployment alerts. Specify your preferred platform: Telegram, WhatsApp, or other.
