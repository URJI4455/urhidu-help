Website Development
Questionnaire Guide

A complete, professional reference for everything URHIDU Company needs from you to build your perfect website — structured clearly, section by section.

Welcome to URHIDU Documentation

This guide walks you through every field in URHIDU Company's website development questionnaire. Each section is designed to help our engineering and design teams understand your organization deeply — so we can build a digital platform that is not just beautiful, but strategically functional and aligned with your exact business goals.

Follow this guide sequentially or jump directly to the section you need using the sidebar navigation.

Before You Begin

Have your organization details, branding assets, and a rough idea of your website goals ready. This will make filling out the questionnaire faster and more accurate.

Documentation Sections

This guide is divided into six structured sections. Each section maps directly to a part of your questionnaire submission.

01

Organizational Profile

Your brand name, type, licensing, founding year, mission, and services.

→
02

Contact Information

Public-facing contact details and internal project administration contacts.

→
03

Website Purpose & Objectives

Primary purpose, problem statement, visitor actions, and payment gateway needs.

04

Target Audience

→
05

Branding & Identity

Logo files, brand colors, typography, taglines, and visual guidelines.

→

General Requirements

Testimonials, social links, Google profile, launch date, budget, and legal pages.

How to Use This Guide

1

Read Each Section Carefully

Each field in the questionnaire has a dedicated documentation page. Read the guidance before filling in your answers to avoid common mistakes.

2

Gather Your Assets

Collect your logo files, brand colors, legal documents, and content before starting. Having everything ready speeds up the process significantly.

3

Submit and Await Review

Once submitted, our team will review your questionnaire within 1–2 business days and reach out to your designated contact for project kick-off.

Important Notice

All information submitted through the URHIDU questionnaire is treated as strictly confidential. Legal and financial details are never shared or displayed publicly without your explicit written consent.

Pro Tip

If you are missing certain details (like a business license number or final logo), write N/A or In Progress. Our team can begin development and integrate those details before launch.
